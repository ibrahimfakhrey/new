History of Mozoon Al Khair Company

A Journey of Success and Achievements Over the Years

The Beginning of the Journey

The story of Mozoon Al Khair Company began with an ambitious vision to provide distinguished services in the field of facility management and integrated services. From the first moment, our goal was clear: to be the trusted partner for companies and institutions in achieving their operational goals.

Over the years, the company has grown and evolved to become one of the leading companies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, with a specialized work team and accumulated experience spanning more than a decade.

Important Milestones in Our History

2008

Foundation and Launch

Mozoon Al Khair Company was established with the aim of providing specialized facility management services, focusing on quality and excellence in service.

2012

First Expansion

The company expanded to include new services and opened additional branches, enabling it to serve a larger number of clients in different regions.

2016

Obtaining Certifications

The company obtained international ISO quality certificates and occupational safety certificates, which enhanced its position in the market.

2020

Digital Transformation

The company invested in modern technologies and smart facility management systems, which improved the efficiency of services provided.

2024

Leadership and Excellence

Today, Mozoon Al Khair Company stands as one of the leading companies in the Kingdom, with a team comprising more than 1000 specialized employees.

Our Legacy and Heritage

Over more than 15 years, Mozoon Al Khair Company has built a strong reputation in the Saudi market as a reliable and innovative company. We are proud of the legacy we have built and the trust our clients have placed in us.

Every project we have executed and every client we have served has contributed to shaping our identity and strengthening our position as leaders in facility management. We carry this legacy with pride and look forward to a brighter future.
